Face Recognition Attendance System

Overview

The Face Recognition Attendance System is an innovative solution designed to automate the attendance tracking process for educational institutions and workplaces. Utilizing advanced facial recognition technology, the system identifies and verifies the presence of teachers and employees in real time.

In a real-world application, a video camera records in the background (classroom, workplace, etc.) while the system performs recognition. For demonstration purposes, we used a webcam.
